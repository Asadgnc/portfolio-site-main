THE STORY

Mehmet Akif Memorial Play

Fall 2022 (11th Grade) School & District Stage
Arts & Leadership

In the first semester of 11th grade, we took on a major project: organizing a theater production in memory of Mehmet Akif Ersoy. This was a significant undertaking, as it was planned for performances both at our school and at the district level.

A Lesson in Dedication

As School President and Literature Club President, I was heavily involved in the organization, working closely with our advisory teachers. Our team of 10-15 students prepared for 2-3 months, holding rehearsals every week. We bought costumes, including the traditional Ottoman Fez, to make the play as authentic as possible.

This was my first-ever theater performance. I was incredibly excited, but as soon as I stepped on stage, the excitement took over, all my nervousness vanished, and I was able to deliver a performance I was proud of.

More Than a Play

That semester was about more than just the play. My role in the club also involved helping to organize several debates and symposiums. We also undertook a complete reorganization of the school library, cleaning the space and sorting all the books to create a better study environment for everyone. It was a busy but incredibly rewarding period of leadership and teamwork.
