THE STORY

The SAT Grind: 1400 in 3 Months

Summer 2025 Municipal Library
Academics & Determination

In the summer of 2025, I made one of the hardest and most sudden decisions of my life: I completely dropped my preparation for the YKS (the Turkish university exam) to focus entirely on the SAT.

With only 4-5 months left before application deadlines, I had to achieve a high score. My English wasn't at an advanced level, so I had to learn the language and master the math section at the same time.

16-Hour Days

My social life vanished. My friend and I dedicated ourselves to a grueling schedule at the municipal library next to our house. We were the first to arrive at 8:30 AM and the last to leave when it closed at 11:45 PM.

We ate our breakfast, lunch, and dinner at the libraryâ€”it provided free meals and internet. The only thing left to do was study. The hardest part wasn't the material, but the sheer physical and mental toll of sitting and focusing for over 14 hours a day.

The Result

That intense, unwavering focus paid off. My innate determination and appetite for learning took over. In just three months of this relentless grind, I took the SAT and scored 1400. It was all worth it.
