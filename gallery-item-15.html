THE STORY

Interfaith Video Project

2022 (11th Grade) High School
Culture & Events

In my 11th grade, our school organized a special religious and cultural event. My contribution was to prepare a video that embodied the event's theme of unity and understanding.

The concept was simple but powerful: to have a single, meaningful sentence spoken in many different languages by students from diverse backgrounds. I reached out to my peers, and we put together a team that included a friend from China and another from Iran, while I represented Uzbekistan.

We worked together to produce the video, which was then shown at the main event. It was a small project, but it carried a large message. I was very pleased to have been part of it and to watch our work resonate with the audience. It remains a very positive memory of collaboration and cultural celebration.
