THE STORY

Teknofest 2023

April 2023 (11th Grade) Atatürk Airport, Istanbul
Technology

During my 11th-grade year, as School President, I helped organize our school's trip to Teknofest 2023. This wasn't just any field trip; it was a massive undertaking. We coordinated several buses to take multiple classes to Turkey's, and one of the world's, largest aviation, space, and technology festivals.

A Glimpse into the Future

Stepping into the festival at Atatürk Airport was like walking into the future. We saw everything: advanced drones (UAVs), rockets, new defense industry hardware, tanks, and innovations in cybersecurity and renewable energy. The entire place was buzzing with energy, competitions, and an incredible exchange of ideas.

Inspiration and Motivation

For me, this trip was transformative. Seeing what we were capable of, especially in aerospace, was deeply motivating. It was at Teknofest that several of my own project ideas began to form. I left the event with a renewed drive and immediately began researching new concepts.

We also saw several political leaders and industry pioneers. Seeing Selçuk Bayraktar, a key figure in Turkey's UAV technology, was a great honor and added to the inspiration of the day.
