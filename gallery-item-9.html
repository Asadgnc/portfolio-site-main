THE STORY

History Talks with Mustafa Armağan

2022 (11th Grade) Municipality Hall & Private Library
History & Learning

In my 11th-grade year, a friend and I discovered an incredible opportunity: our municipality was organizing a 5-6 week series of "History Talks" every Friday night. The speaker was Mustafa Armağan, a prominent and often controversial Turkish historian known for his deep dives into Ottoman history.

An Unforgettable Experience

These weren't typical lectures. Each session lasted at least three hours, filled with mesmerizing stories about Ottoman history that you wouldn't find in a standard textbook. We were completely captivated and never felt bored. We asked questions, learned about historical debates, and saw history from a new perspective.

The most amazing part was when Mr. Armağan invited attendees to sessions in his own home—specifically, in his massive private library. Seeing that collection was astonishing. It was filled with incredibly old books, including original Ottoman manuscripts in a different alphabet. It felt like touching a piece of history.

My Takeaway

This experience profoundly changed my view of history and deepened my love for it. Mr. Armağan's passion was infectious. Since then, I've read many of his books. He is a historian I respect greatly, and I am thankful for the opportunity to have learned from him directly in such a unique and personal setting.
