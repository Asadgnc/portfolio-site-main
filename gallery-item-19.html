THE STORY

Recitation at the TÜGVA Dinner

March 2023 (11th Grade) Ümraniye Evlendirme Sarayı, Istanbul
Culture & Performance

In my 11th-grade year, I was given a truly great honor. The Turkish Youth Foundation (TÜGVA) held a major evening dinner event at the Ümraniye Evlendirme Sarayı, and I was invited to perform the opening Quran recitation.

A High-Profile Stage

This was not a small school event. It was a grand hall filled with state officials and prominent political leaders, including the Mayor of Ümraniye, İsmet Yıldırım, and (if I recall correctly) the former Prime Minister, Binali Yıldırım.

Reciting on such a large stage, in front of so many important figures, was one of the most significant and exciting moments of my high school life. It was a huge test of my confidence and ability to perform under pressure, and I was deeply honored to have been given the opportunity.
