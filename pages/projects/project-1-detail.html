UniVerse Turkiye

A comprehensive digital platform designed to centralize all information for high school and university students in Turkey, simplifying the complex university selection process.

The Challenge

Currently, high school and university students in Turkey must gather information from dozens of fragmented sources—university websites, government portals, and private counseling centers. This process is often overwhelming, inefficient, and creates an inequality of access to vital information.

My Solution

This platform will serve as the single source of truth for all things related to higher education in Turkey. It is planned to be built on two core pillars:

1. A Comprehensive Information Hub

A dynamic database containing detailed, up-to-date information on every university in Turkey, including admission requirements, program curricula, campus life, and scholarship opportunities.

2. Integrated Guidance & Counseling

An expert-driven counseling service dedicated to providing strategic advice, study plans, and support for the national university entrance exams (YKS) and other academic challenges.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5
  • CSS3 (Flexbox & Grid)
  • JavaScript

Project Goals

  • Demonstrate JS logic
  • Create a useful educational tool
