THE STORY

School Iftar Recitation

April 19, 2022 (11th Grade) High School Campus
Culture & Performance

During the month of Ramadan in my 11th-grade year, our school hosted a massive Iftar dinner. This was a comprehensive event, with tables set up in the largest open area of our school, bringing together hundreds of students, parents, and all of our teachers.

A Shared Honor

As was becoming a tradition, the duty of performing the opening Quran recitation for this large-scale event fell to me. This time, I had the pleasure of sharing the stage with my friend, Mehmet Akif, who also recited.

We both helped manage the flow of this beautiful evening, and the reception from the crowd was incredibly warm. Hearing the applause was a great feeling. Every time I participated in an event like this, my motivation grew stronger. It was another wonderful memory of community, faith, and shared celebration.
