Hobby & Health

Alongside all my academic and event activities, I've always maintained a commitment to fitness. I've trained at various intervals, building good muscle mass and improving my physique. But the benefits have been far more than just physical.

More Than a Hobby

Committing to fitness is a responsibility. It's a transformative process that has had a comprehensive impact on my life. My sleep schedule improved. My motivation skyrocketed. I felt happier, healthier, and more planned in my daily life.

It taught me a new level of discipline, from managing my time to eating healthier. I truly believe that engaging in sports is essential; it changes you for the better. This passion for fitness has also pushed me to explore other areas, like martial arts, which I am also getting into.
