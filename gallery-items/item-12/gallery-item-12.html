THE STORY

Student of the Month

October 2022 (11th Grade) High School
Achievements

In my 11th-grade year, right as the school year was getting into full swing, I was selected as the "Student of the Month" for October.

Full Speed Ahead

This award was given for exemplary conduct both inside and outside of school, as well as for my consistent and active participation in school events. I had started the year at full speed ("tam gaz basıyordum"), making a conscious effort to be involved in every event, competition, and activity available.

Being School President already came with high expectations, but I wanted to prove that I could handle those responsibilities while still being an active and engaged member of the student community. This award felt like a direct validation of that effort.

A Motivational Boost

Receiving this recognition so early in the year was incredibly motivating. It wasn't a major trophy, but it was a sign that my efforts were being noticed by the faculty and my peers. It energized me and reinforced my commitment to stay involved and make a positive impact throughout the rest of my time in high school.
