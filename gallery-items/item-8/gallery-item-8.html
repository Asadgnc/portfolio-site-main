THE STORY

A Trip to Çamlıca Mosque

Spring 2023 (11th Grade) Çamlıca Hill, Istanbul
Culture & Events

In the spring of my 11th-grade year, as School President, I organized a trip to take our students to Çamlıca Mosque, the largest mosque in Europe. This wasn't just a religious site, but also a center of art, culture, and history.

A Breathtaking View

The Çamlıca Mosque is an architectural marvel, the largest in Europe, but it's also built on the highest point in all of Istanbul. When we arrived, we were left speechless. You could see the entire 16-million-person city, the Bosphorus strait, and both major bridges. The view was almost overwhelming.

We also explored the vast complex inside. We learned about the symbolism in its architecture, visited the grand library (and even sat down to study for a bit), and toured the Museum of Islamic Civilizations located within the mosque. It was incredible to see so many historical artifacts from the Ottoman era and various Arab countries.
