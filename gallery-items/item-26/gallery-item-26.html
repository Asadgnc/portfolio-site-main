THE JOURNEY

Hankando: Turkish Martial Art

2022 - 2024 Hankando Academy, Istanbul
Hobby & Discipline

In 11th grade, after reaching a certain level of physical fitness, I realized I needed to channel my strength into martial arts. My research led me to Hankando, a new and practical Turkish martial art. Although I was initially skeptical about its novelty, I was intrigued by its reputation.

I found Mustafa Hoca in Istanbul. At that time, there was no official gym—just a tiny 15-20 square meter room where he began teaching our group of five young students.

Why Hankando?

I quickly realized Hankando wasn't just for show; it was highly effective, similar to Systema, designed to fill the gaps found in other global martial arts. It was practical and direct.

Impressed by the first session, I committed instantly. We trained in that small room for nearly a year. It was an intense period where we built the foundation of our skills.

Growth and Leadership

Eventually, our instructor decided to open a proper academy. As his "Assistant Student," I was involved in every stage of the organization, from the setup to the grand opening. Even my father joined the classes, making it a family effort.

Once the academy opened, I served as an assistant instructor for a year. I taught new students while continuing my own education, passing exams, and earning higher belt ranks.

A Lasting Impact

By the end of this journey, I felt genuinely self-confident and free of fear, knowing I could defend myself effectively. However, Hankando is a powerful weapon that can be dangerous in the wrong hands, so we maintained a strict policy of only enrolling people with good character.

This sport gave us more than just physical skills; its philosophy matured us, developed our self-esteem, and profoundly changed our perspective on life.
