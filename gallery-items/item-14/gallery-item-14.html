THE STORY

Republic Day Celebration

October 29, 2022 (11th Grade) High School
Events & Leadership

For the 99th anniversary of the Turkish Republic (Cumhuriyet Bayramı), our school planned a major 10-day celebration. As School President, I was part of the 7-8 person team responsible for organizing and performing in the main event.

Honoring the Republic

October 29th is a profound date in Turkish history, marking the proclamation of the republic in 1923. Our goal was to create an event that truly honored this legacy. We held several discussions and poetry readings about the importance of the republic.

My Role

My role was both organizational and performative. I helped coordinate the student team and also took the stage to recite poems and deliver a speech about the meaning of the day. Our main performance was a powerful rendition of the Turkish National Anthem (İstiklal Marşı).

A Proud Moment

It was a proud moment to stand with my team and lead the school in celebrating such an important day. This event reinforced my skills in public speaking and event management under a tight deadline.
