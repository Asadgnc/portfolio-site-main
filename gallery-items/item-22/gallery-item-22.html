THE STORY

Vakti Kuşanmak Project

January 11, 2023 (11th Grade) High School
Learning & Culture

On January 11, 2023, our school hosted an event called the "Vakti Kuşanmak" (Girding Time) Project. This was an in-school conference led by two esteemed teachers, Hüseyin Arıcı and Hüseyin Hatipoğlu.

An Evening of Knowledge

I was invited to participate by opening the event with a recitation of the Quran and its Turkish meaning (meal). Following the recitation, the hosts delivered a deeply meaningful talk, touching on important subjects.

The floor was then opened for a Q&A session, and we as students had the chance to ask our questions and receive insightful answers. It was truly a night dedicated to acquiring knowledge ("ilim") and was incredibly beneficial for everyone who attended.

My Takeaway

Being part of this event was a great honor. It was a perfect blend of spiritual opening and intellectual discussion, and I was happy to contribute to such a valuable evening for my school community.
