THE STORY

Nasheed Performance

2021 (10th Grade) High School
Arts & Performance

In my 10th-grade year, I joined a cultural event to perform traditional Nasheeds (Islamic spiritual songs). I've had a good voice since I was young and participated in music events before, but this was a new challenge I wanted to take on to truly test my vocal abilities.

A Month of Dedication

Our team consisted of three friends. We practiced for a full month, memorizing multiple nasheeds and perfecting our harmonies. It took a lot of dedication.

When the day of the event arrived, our performance was a success. We received wonderful applause from the audience. I was so happy to have been part of this experience; it gave me a newfound awareness and confidence in my voice.
