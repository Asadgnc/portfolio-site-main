THE STORY

Return to Çanakkale

Spring 2024 (12th Grade) Çanakkale, Turkey
Events & History

In my 12th-grade year, I had the privilege of returning to Çanakkale. Just as our school did every year, we organized the trip in partnership with the municipality. This time, as a senior and still serving as School President, I was able to join my own class for this profound journey.

Deeper Understanding

Visiting for the second time allowed for an even deeper experience. We were able to see places we had missed on the first trip, giving me a more complete picture of the sheer scale of the Gallipoli Campaign.

The Gallipoli Campaign (1915-1916) was a defining moment in modern Turkish history. Seeing the actual trenches, the memorials, and the models of battle tactics, I felt like I was living through history. It's a place of incredible sacrifice and resilience, a testament to the human spirit that secured Turkey's future.

Lasting Memories

The trip was filled with every emotion—pride, sadness, joy, and a deep sense of awareness. We didn't just tour the sites; we lived them. We created lasting memories with friends, visited local restaurants, and even had long history discussions with our teachers, debating the tactics and outcomes of the war.

A Changed Perspective

This visit reaffirmed my belief that it is a crucial experience for every student. It truly changed my worldview and gave me a profound appreciation for the sacrifices made. I took countless photos, trying to capture the feeling of the place.
