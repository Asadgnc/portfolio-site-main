THE STORY

The Çanakkale (Gallipoli) Trip

Spring 2023 (11th Grade) Çanakkale, Turkey
Events

In my 11th-grade year, our school organized its annual trip to Çanakkale in partnership with the municipality. This trip was traditionally reserved for 12th graders, but as School President, I had been deeply involved in helping organize the logistics. As a result, I was granted the special opportunity to join them.

A Journey into History

It was my first time visiting Çanakkale. As we toured the historical sites, I was amazed. The traces of history were so real, so present, it felt as if I was reliving that time. We visited the famous monuments and battlefields of the Gallipoli Campaign, a pivotal victory that helped shape Turkey's independence.

A Mix of Emotions

I experienced every emotion imaginable: I was moved, I was happy, I was sad, I laughed. The trip wasn't just a tour; it was a profound experience. We saw models of the battle tactics and the soldiers' living quarters—some were replicas, but many were the actual, preserved sites. Standing there, I felt a deep connection to the past.

A Changed Perspective

I left with no regrets and would go again in a heartbeat. Sending students to that place is such a logical and important decision. Being there, walking those grounds, truly changed my worldview. It's one thing to read about history in a book, but it's another thing entirely to stand where it happened.
