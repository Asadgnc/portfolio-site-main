THE STORY

10th Grade Valedictorian

June 2021 School Auditorium
Achievements

This was a significant milestone for me. Throughout the 10th grade, I worked incredibly hard. There was a friendly but intense rivalry with another girl in my class who was always competing for the top spot. It was a silent, almost imaginary competition, but it pushed me to give my absolute best.

The Moment

On awards day, in front of all the students, I was announced as the 10th Grade Valedictorian with a score of 93.8375. I won our "secret competition."

It wasn't my first time on a stage, so the excitement I felt wasn't from stage fright. It was from pure, overwhelming happiness at achieving a goal I had dedicated so much to. I clearly remember my legs shaking—not from fear, but from the simple, profound joy of that moment. It was a validation of all the hard work.
