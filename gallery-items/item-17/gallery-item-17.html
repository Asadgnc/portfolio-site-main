THE STORY

Quran Recitation Competition

2022 (11th Grade) School & District
Achievements

In 11th grade, I participated in the school's Quran Recitation competition. After dedicated preparation, I won 1st place in my school, which earned me the honor of advancing to the district-level competition.

A Proud Moment

I had to prove the quality of my voice and recitation to everyone, so I worked very hard. At the district competition, I competed against winners from many other schools and successfully earned a high-ranking position (derece elde ettim).

It was a beautiful event filled with memorable moments. We received valuable gifts and books as awards, and several well-known figures gave speeches. My father also came with me to the competition, and I could see how proud he was. It was a wonderful and validating experience.
