THE HOBBY

My Hobby: Billiards

2020 - Present Billiards Halls
Hobby

For the past four years, billiards has been more than just a game to me; it's been a consistent hobby that I've grown to love. I play frequently with friends and believe I've reached a near-professional level in my skills.

A Game of Skill

I've particularly mastered the American (Pool) and 3-Ball (3-Cushion) variants. I love this hobby because it's not just about hitting a ball. It's a game of calculation.

To play well, you must constantly make geometric calculations, predict angles, and measure distances by eye. This mental challenge, combined with the physical precision, makes it an incredibly fun and rewarding way to sharpen the mind.

More Than a Hobby
